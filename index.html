Suicide Awareness

Suicide is an important probeom that affects the person and their loved ones. It can affect families and communities but it can also make the person experiencing issues with their mental health. So we think that there should be more supportive envioroments for people to be able to access help when they needed. We can work together to prevent suicide and support those in need. If you or someone is struggling here are some resources that may help you:

Suicide signs vary and every person deals in a different way. You can noticing you or your love ones are experiencing suicidal thoughts by:

  1. Active listening
  2. Having empathy

Warning signs include:

  1. Talking about suicide
  2. Changes in behavior
  3. Loss of interest
  4. Giving away belongnins
  5. Saying goodbye
  6. Hopelessness

Causes of Suicide

Suicide can be causes by many ways like getting bullied physically and online or sometimes by feeling like you dont fit in in society. Another cause, can include the following:

  1. Neural system imbalance
  2. Hormonal changes
  3. Trauma
  4. Stress
  5. Social isolation
  6. Negative thinking partterns
  7. coping mechanisms
  8. Chronic illness
  9. Substance abuse
